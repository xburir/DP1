Logout

To logout, click on the user button and then click on the logout button.

Upload files

Click on the upload button

Upload only a zip file, which contains directories "Walk" and "Drive". The zip can contain both, or just one of the mentioned directories.

ZIP Structure:

            |-- Drive
            |   |-- 33718.csv
            |   |-- 31783.geojson
            |-- Walk
            |   |-- 93293.csv
            |   |-- 44352.csv
            |   |-- 764555.geojson
            |   |-- 354852.csv

Show unsuccessful uploads

If the server can't unzip your zip, the zip will be stored on the server for later download.

If your routes can't be map-matched, the zip and the unzipped files will stay on the server for later try to map-match.

If there are any problems with the uploaded files, there will be a notification on the show list of errors button.

Click on the show errors button

After clicking the button, you will see a dialog:

You can download your zip file by clicking on the download zip icon:

After clicking the icon, map-matching will be run again.

Display routes

Click on the show routes icon.

After that a dialog box will be displayed.

You will see some information about your uploaded file. You can choose to display the original route(red) or the map-matched one(blue).
